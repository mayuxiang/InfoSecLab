## Ongoing Research Projects

### 1. AI-Driven Network Intrusion Detection

Developing transformer-based deep learning models for real-time network traffic analysis and anomaly detection. Our approach achieves state-of-the-art accuracy on benchmark datasets with significantly reduced false positive rates.

**Keywords:** Deep Learning, Network Traffic Classification, Anomaly Detection

---

### 2. Post-Quantum Cryptographic Schemes

Investigating lattice-based and code-based cryptographic constructions that remain secure against quantum computing attacks. We focus on practical efficiency and formal security proofs.

**Keywords:** Lattice Cryptography, Code-Based Crypto, Quantum Resistance

---

### 3. Privacy-Preserving Federated Learning

Designing federated learning frameworks with formal differential privacy guarantees, enabling collaborative model training across multiple parties without exposing raw data.

**Keywords:** Federated Learning, Differential Privacy, Distributed ML

---

### 4. Adversarial Robustness in Deep Learning

Studying adversarial attack and defense mechanisms in deep neural networks, with applications to security-critical systems such as autonomous driving and medical imaging.

**Keywords:** Adversarial Examples, Robustness Certification, Defense Strategies

---

### 5. Embodied Intelligence for Autonomous Systems

Researching safe and reliable embodied AI systems, including sim-to-real transfer, robot learning from demonstration, and safety verification for autonomous agents.

**Keywords:** Robotics, Sim-to-Real, Safety Verification, Reinforcement Learning

---

### 6. Blockchain Security & Smart Contract Auditing

Analyzing smart contract vulnerabilities and developing automated tools for detecting security flaws in decentralized applications.

**Keywords:** Smart Contracts, Formal Verification, Vulnerability Detection
