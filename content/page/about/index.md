## About the Lab

The **Information Security LAB** (IS Lab) is a research group at **Henan University** dedicated to advancing the frontiers of cybersecurity and intelligent systems.

### Our Mission

To conduct impactful research at the intersection of **information security**, **artificial intelligence**, and **embodied intelligence**, training the next generation of researchers and contributing to a safer, smarter digital world.

### Research Directions

| Area | Focus |
|------|-------|
| 🔐 **Network Security** | Intrusion detection, secure protocols, SDN/NFV security |
| 🧮 **Cryptography** | Post-quantum schemes, homomorphic encryption, blockchain security |
| 🤖 **AI for Security** | Deep learning for malware analysis, adversarial robustness, threat intelligence |
| 🛡️ **Privacy Engineering** | Differential privacy, federated learning, secure multi-party computation |
| 🦾 **Embodied Intelligence** | Robot perception, sim-to-real transfer, safety-critical autonomous systems |
| 🔍 **Software Security** | Vulnerability analysis, binary reverse engineering, automated exploit generation |

### Environment

Based in **Kaifeng, Henan, China** — one of China's most historic cities and home to Henan University's vibrant academic community.

### Collaborations

We actively collaborate with leading research groups across China and internationally. Prospective partners are welcome to [reach out](/page/contact/).
